What We Do:

The goal of this application is to promote a cheap and accessible way for students to buy and sell textbooks. Stop getting ripped off by the bookstore and start exchanging books here! We dont actually deal with the transaction. All we do is provide a platform for students to upload books and get in contact with sellers. Once you find the book you want, its up to you to agree on price, and when/where to meet. Enjoy.

Buy!

In order to buy textbooks, click on the 'Buy' button above. We don't actually deal with the textbook transaction. We just put you in contact with a seller and then you can negotiate.

Sell!

Looking to sell a textbook? Upload your book's information on the Bates Textbook Exchange! If someone is looking to buy your book, they will reach out to you. Its your guys job to negotiate price, where to meet, and when to meet.
